Mission

Research the prevention and treatment of anxiety-related disorders. Question all assumptions, leverage new technologies, and collaborate with adjacent fields of study.

Ongoing Research & Future Directions

  • Translational research, from basic science to the clinic
    Leveraging cutting edge findings on mechanisms of fear reduction in animal models to improve exposure therapy outcomes for anxiety-related disorders (i.e., promoting reconsolidation update versus new inhibitory learning)
  • Gaming
    Drawing on the use of reward and immersion in virtual-reality game play to improve engagement in, and reduce dropout from, exposure based therapies.
  • Neurofeedback
    Use of neurofeedback during exposure therapy to facilitate mechanisms of fear reduction.
  • Dissemination
    Improving dissemination of cognitive-behavioral therapies for anxiety related disorders via web-based applications, 3D video, and gaming systems. Identifying and targeting therapist-related barriers to implementation of exposure-based techniques.

Lab Director
